  - Got stuck: couldn't prove escape from n ≡ 7, 15, 31,... (mod 2^k)

Our approach:
  - Shifted to BIT-LEVEL analysis (trailing ones τ(n))
  - Discovery: τ decreases monotonically in v=1 regime
  - Result: Logarithmic bound on v=1 streaks, ruling out exponential divergence

WHY IT WORKS:
  - τ(n) captures global binary structure, not just residue mod 2^k
  - Monotonic decrease forces escape when τ reaches 1
  - Bounds streak length by log(n), independent of specific residue class
