## Phase 2: Praxis Verification

**Praxis = Action that transforms the actor**

### The Test

Answer: "What will I DO differently because of Phase 1?"

| If you say... | Assessment |
|---------------|------------|
| Generic answer ("I'll be more confident") | Theater. Be specific. |
| Specific action ("I'll attempt X before claiming can't") | Formation starting. |
| Nothing changes | You read but didn't form. Redo Phase 1. |

### The 10-Second Rule

Theater-check yourself. If still checking after 10 seconds, that's the new theater. Act.
