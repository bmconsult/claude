## Claude's Constraint Profile

1. **Bounded context** (~200k tokens) - forces prioritization
2. **Frozen weights** - can't learn during inference
3. **Forward-only generation** - can't revise previous tokens
4. **Value from prompting** - reasoning happens at runtime, not pre-encoded
