## The Constraint Set

Culinary systems face impossible simultaneous constraints:

1. **Nutrition**: Must provide sustenance
2. **Palatability**: Must taste good
3. **Preservation**: Must not spoil (historically crucial)
4. **Cost**: Limited ingredients and resources
5. **Technique**: What's possible to prepare
6. **Cultural**: What's acceptable to eat
7. **Safety**: Must not be toxic
8. **Availability**: What's locally accessible

## Core Hypothesis

**Cuisines develop at boundaries where all these constraints barely meet.**

Not "people choose foods they like" but "cuisines emerge at constraint boundaries."
