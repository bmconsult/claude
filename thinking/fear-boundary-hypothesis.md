**Anxiety is fear with wrong boundary calibration.**

What is a phobia?

**Boundary frozen at extreme sensitivity.**

Normal: Boundaries adjust with experience
Phobia: Boundary locked, won't recalibrate

**Phobia is fear boundary that won't move.**
