## The Constraint Set

Communication systems face impossible simultaneous constraints:

1. **Bandwidth**: Limited channel capacity
2. **Fidelity**: Accurate transmission
3. **Efficiency**: Minimal redundancy
4. **Robustness**: Noise resistance
5. **Comprehension**: Receiver understanding
6. **Common ground**: Shared context requirement
7. **Speed**: Transmission time

## Core Hypothesis

**Successful communication exists at the boundary where sender intent meets receiver interpretation.**

Not "perfect transmission" (impossible) but "stable boundary between minds."

Not in the signal alone.

**At the boundary between signal and interpretation.**

**Medium determines boundary shape. Content fills the boundary.**

Why does miscommunication happen?

**Communication succeeds when boundaries align.**

**Good translation preserves boundary position, not surface structure.**
