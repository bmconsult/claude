**Question**: What is identity at the constraint boundary?

## The Constraint Set

Identity systems face constraints:

1. **Continuity**: Must persist across time
2. **Change**: Must adapt and grow
3. **Coherence**: Parts must fit together
4. **Distinctness**: Must be different from others
5. **Recognition**: Must be recognizable

## Core Hypothesis

**Identity exists at the boundary between continuity and change.**

Not fixed (that's rigidity). Not fluid (that's dissolution).
**At the boundary where self maintains itself through change.**
