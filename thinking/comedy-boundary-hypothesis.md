Humor operates at boundaries between:

1. **Expectation**: What we predict

## Core Hypothesis

**Humor exists at the boundary between expectation and safe violation.**

Not "surprising" (too unexpected = confusing).

**At the boundary: predictable enough to follow, surprising enough to violate.**
