## The Constraint Set

Relationships face impossible constraints:

1. **Autonomy**: Each person needs independence
2. **Connection**: Relationship requires interdependence
3. **Honesty**: Truth-telling
4. **Kindness**: Not hurting
5. **Growth**: Personal development
6. **Stability**: Relationship continuity

## Core Hypothesis

**Healthy relationships exist at the boundary between autonomy and connection.**

Not too close (enmeshment). Not too far (disconnection).
**At the boundary where two people meet.**
