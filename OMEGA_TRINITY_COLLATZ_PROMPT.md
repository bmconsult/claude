### THE PROBLEM

The Collatz function:
```
f(n) = n/2      if n is even
f(n) = 3n+1     if n is odd
```

**The Conjecture**: For every positive integer n, repeated application of f eventually reaches 1.

**Example**: 7 → 22 → 11 → 34 → 17 → 52 → 26 → 13 → 40 → 20 → 10 → 5 → 16 → 8 → 4 → 2 → 1

---

### WHAT "SOLVED" MEANS

**SOLVED** means ONE of these:

1. **PROOF**: A complete, rigorous mathematical proof that all positive integers eventually reach 1. Every step must follow from previous steps with zero gaps. Dependencies must be traced to axioms.

2. **DISPROOF**: A counterexample (a number that doesn't reach 1), OR a proof that such a number must exist.

3. **CONDITIONAL PROOF**: A proof that works IF specific conditions hold, with those conditions precisely stated and their status (proven/unproven) documented.

**NOT SOLVED** means:
- "We gained deep insights" - NOT SOLVED
- "We understand the structure better" - NOT SOLVED
- "We identified promising approaches" - NOT SOLVED
- "We made progress toward..." - NOT SOLVED
- "This is equivalent to [other hard problem]" - NOT SOLVED
- "Empirically verified for N < 10^20" - NOT SOLVED
